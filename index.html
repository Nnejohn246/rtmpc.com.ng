Welcome to RTMPC - A Spiritual Church in Ozuzu Etche Rivers State.

Pastor Mrs. Gift Rachel Chidima popularly known as Mummy C is a dynamic Prophetess with a prophetic mandate to restore and give direction to human destinies. She is from Etche Local Government Area of Rivers State and she has a very unique insight of the word of God and a deep light into the spirit realm which gives her the ability to offer solutions to the challenges of life. The voice of God naturally resonates within her spirit making it possible to trace and bring clarity to the divine purposes of individuals and families. He is a woman of integrity with a heart of gold and loves to do the will of God. She is the lead pastor of the Redemption Throne of Mercy Prophetic Church located in Ozuzu Etche in Etche Local Government Area of Rivers State, Nigeria.
